# About Me

> Code, websites, and the odd bit of magic

Hello, I’m Connor, a freelancer, film-maker, CTO and Software Engineer from Sheffield, Yorkshire (UK). I build websites, web apps, games, and software projects. I’ve worked in the traffic and hospitality industries, but I also have a background in digital media, particularly live event multi-camera productions and documentaries.


I’m used to working independently or as part of a team, picking up projects at any stage and seeing them through. I’ve also pitched projects to Amazon, Microsoft, and other big names at GDC London, which was quite an experience! A lot of my work is under strict NDAs, which means my clients trust me with their sensitive and high-profile projects - and I take that trust seriously.


If you need something built, improved, or just finished off, let’s have a chat!
